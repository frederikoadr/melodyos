Music Elements Glossary

Musical Note

a symbol denoting a musical sound. Notes can represent the pitch and duration of a sound in musical notation.

Melody

A succession of notes, varying in pitch, which have an organized and recognizable shape. Melody also have scale.

Music Scale

formally recognized as systems of tones from which melody can be built and contribute to its overall character.
